Important disclaimer.

Bank Harm Registry provides educational, organizational, public-data, and consent-management tools. It does not determine liability or prove that any institution harmed any person.

User-submitted reports

Reports describe a submitter’s stated experience. They are not adjudicated findings and must not be presented as verified wrongdoing without independent support.

Public data

CFPB complaint data and regulatory actions provide context. Complaint counts are not necessarily representative of all consumers and do not prove an individual claim.

AI-generated material

Any AI-assisted narrative, route suggestion, score, declaration, or evidence summary may be incomplete or wrong. Verify facts, law, jurisdiction, deadlines, and citations independently.

Declarations

A declaration should be truthful, based on personal knowledge, limited to facts the signer can support, and reviewed for the governing court or agency.

Volunteer and expert support

Volunteer participation does not create expert status. Any expert role depends on qualifications, reliability, conflicts, governing rules, and case-specific approval.

Legal advice

Nothing on this site substitutes for advice from a licensed attorney or other qualified professional.
